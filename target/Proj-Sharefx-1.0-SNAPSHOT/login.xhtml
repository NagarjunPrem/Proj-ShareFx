Stfx-ShareFx

Sharing is an act of love towards the whole of humanity that makes a better society. ShareFx is a unique platform for the members of StFx Society to share their knowledge, skills, hobbies, rides and food. This application website acts as an interface between students and other members who are willing to share a resource or a skill. By this platform, this project aims to bring the StFx society closer and develop better human relationships, breaking the cultural and communication barrier.
